InvestBuddy

Professional investment education platform designed for beginners. Learn chart analysis, trading patterns, and investment strategies through interactive simulations and personalized guidance.

In Development - Launching 2026

Democratizing Investment Education

InvestBuddy bridges the gap between complex financial markets and everyday investors. Our platform provides comprehensive investment education through interactive chart analysis, historical market simulations, and AI-powered personalized guidance tailored for the Korean market.

Core Features

Interactive Chart Education

Learn chart patterns, technical indicators, and trend analysis through guided tutorials and hands-on practice with professional-grade charting tools.

Historical Market Simulation

Experience major market events like the 2020 COVID crash through time-machine simulations. Practice trading in historical contexts with real market data.

AI-Powered Personalization

Receive customized investment strategies based on your risk tolerance, investment goals, and learning progress through advanced AI algorithms.

Investment Style Analysis

Discover your investment personality through comprehensive assessment and get tailored education content that matches your learning style and risk preferences.

Risk-Free Learning Environment

Practice with virtual money in a completely safe environment. Make mistakes, learn from them, and build confidence before real investing.

Mobile-First Design

Learn anywhere, anytime with our native Android application optimized for mobile learning and professional chart analysis on the go.
